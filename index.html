Welcome

This encyclopaedia is a reference for the faithful seeking to understand traditional Catholic doctrine — the sacraments, indulgences, sin, the Traditional Latin Mass, and commonly misunderstood teachings of the Church. All content draws from the perennial Magisterium, the Council of Trent, the Roman Catechism, papal encyclicals, and the Summa Theologica of St. Thomas Aquinas. All Scripture citations use the Douay-Rheims translation.

"The truth is like a lion; you don't have to defend it. Let it loose; it will defend itself." — St. Augustine

How to Use This Site

Use the navigation sidebar to explore the encyclopaedia:

  • The Seven Sacraments — Baptism, Confirmation, Holy Eucharist, Penance, Extreme Unction, Holy Orders, and Matrimony: their matter, form, effects, and conditions.
  • Indulgences — The Church's treasury of merit, conditions for gaining indulgences, and the most common indulgenced prayers with their texts.
  • Types of Sin — A thorough examination of mortal and venial sin, organized by the Ten Commandments and the Seven Deadly Sins.
  • Church Teachings Clarified — Clear explanations of doctrines often misunderstood or misrepresented, from the Real Presence to Papal Infallibility.
  • Traditional Latin Mass Guide — What to expect at the TLM, the parts of the Mass, server responses, Communion etiquette, and key texts.
  • Saints & Devotions — The Communion of Saints, the Holy Rosary, the Brown Scapular, sacramentals, patron saints, and litanies.
  • Apologetics FAQ — Scripture and Church Father answers to common Protestant objections: Sola Scriptura, Sola Fide, Mary, Purgatory, the Papacy, and more.
