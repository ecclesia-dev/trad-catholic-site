About

We are a group of traditional Catholic developers building free tools for the faith — Scripture readers, breviaries, rosaries, and more. We do not monetize prayer or the Word of God. Every project is offered freely to the Church.

Ad maiorem Dei gloriam.

Projects

  • DRB Bible [iOS] — Douay-Rheims Bible with Haydock & Lapide commentaries.
  • Rosary [iOS] — Traditional guided Rosary and novenas.
  • Gospel RPG [iOS] — Scripture-based pixel art RPG for kids.
  • Martyrology [CLI] — Roman Martyrology for your terminal.
  • Lectionary [CLI] — Traditional lectionary readings.
  • Summa [CLI] — St. Thomas Aquinas’s Summa Theologiae in the terminal.
  • opus [CLI] — Traditional Divine Office / Breviary. (coming soon)

Nostr

Find us on Nostr — the censorship-resistant protocol. We run two bots: Daily Aquinas, posting a question and reply from the Summa each day, and Divinum Officium, sharing the hours of the Divine Office. Search for ecclesiadev on any Nostr client.
